Community Guidelines

Last updated: 30 May 2026

Saanjh is a space for genuine, respectful, marriage-minded connections. Using the app means agreeing to these guidelines. We have zero tolerance for objectionable content and abusive users.

Be real

  • Use your real photos and accurate information.
  • No impersonation, catfishing, or fake profiles.
  • Complete the video verification honestly.

Be respectful

  • No harassment, hate speech, threats, or discrimination.
  • No sexually explicit or nude content.
  • No violent, graphic, or illegal content.

Keep it genuine

  • No spam, solicitation, scams, or asking for money.
  • No promoting other services or moving people off-platform to defraud them.
  • Saanjh is for adults 18+ only.

Reporting & enforcement

You can report or block any member from their profile or your chat. We review every report and remove offending content or users — typically within 24 hours. Serious violations result in immediate, permanent removal. Repeatedly reported profiles are automatically hidden pending review.

If you ever feel unsafe, contact us at safety@saanjh.app and, in an emergency, your local authorities.
