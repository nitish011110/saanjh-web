Common questions

How do I sign in?

Enter your email and we'll send a 6-digit code. Type it in to sign in — no password needed.

How does verification work?

A quick live face check confirms you're a real person. Verified profiles get a badge.

How do I report or block someone?

Open their profile or your chat, tap the menu, and choose Report or Block. We review every report and act within 24 hours.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through Apple. Open the Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → Saanjh to change or cancel. Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal to avoid the next charge.

How do I delete my account?

In Saanjh, go to the You tab → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, photos, matches, and messages.
