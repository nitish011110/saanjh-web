Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

This policy explains what Saanjh collects, why, and your choices. Saanjh is operated by Shivraj Trading LLC ("Saanjh", "we", "us").

Information we collect

  • Account & contact: your email address (used to sign in via a one-time code).
  • Profile content: photos, an optional video prompt, text-prompt answers, and details you choose to add — name, date of birth, height, gender, who you're interested in, city/country, community, ethnicity, religion, languages, diet, education, profession, and family background.
  • Preferences: your match and discovery settings (age range, distance, and deal-breakers).
  • Approximate location: city-level coordinates derived from the city you enter, used only to show distance-based matches. We do not collect precise GPS location.
  • Messages & activity: messages with your matches, likes/passes, RSVPs to events, and reports or blocks you submit.
  • Identifiers: a user ID and, if you enable notifications, a device push token.

Sensitive information

Some details you may add (such as religion, ethnicity, and who you are interested in) can be considered sensitive. You provide these voluntarily to power matching, and we process them only to operate the service. You can edit or remove them at any time.

How we use your information

  • To create your profile and show it to other members in discovery.
  • To match you with people who fit your stated preferences and intent.
  • To enable messaging, events, and the optional family-share preview.
  • To keep Saanjh safe — verification, reviewing reports, and removing abusive content or users.
  • To send service notifications (e.g. new matches or messages) if you opt in.

How we share information

  • With other members: your profile card (photos, prompts, and the details you make visible) is shown to other members. Exact age, exact location, and your preferences are never shown.
  • Family-share: only if you generate a share link; it shows a read-only preview and can be revoked by you.
  • Service providers: we use Supabase (database, authentication, storage) and Apple (push notifications, in-app purchases) to run the app. We do not sell your data.
  • Legal: we may disclose information if required by law or to protect users' safety.

Tracking & advertising

Saanjh does not track you across other apps or websites and does not use your data for third-party advertising.

Your choices & rights

  • Edit or hide profile fields anytime in the app.
  • Delete your account from Settings — this removes your profile, photos, matches, and messages.
  • Request a copy of your data or ask questions by emailing privacy@saanjh.app.

Data retention

We keep your information while your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data, except where we must retain limited records to comply with law or resolve disputes.

Children

Saanjh is only for people 18 and older. We do not knowingly allow anyone under 18 to use the service.

Changes

We may update this policy; we'll post the new date above and, for material changes, notify you in the app.
